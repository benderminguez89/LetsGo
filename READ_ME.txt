Have you ever wanted to find and plan group outings based on mutual interests
LetsGO is a web application designed to faciliated just that

Users provide a profile of their interests, from types of events(sporting, converts, conventions, etc.) 

Events will be recommended to them based on their interests and history of activity

Users are alerted when one of their friends has a shared interest/event

Users can also opt to find new friends based on their interests
